Executive Summary

  1. project44 landed inside the SAP ecosystem. On June 16, project44's Real-Time Multi-Modal Visibility Data became an SAP Endorsed App on the SAP Store, feeding SAP Business Network for Logistics live carrier signal across ocean, rail, drayage, air & parcel — a direct distribution threat into the procurement stack of Gnosis's largest BCO targets.

  5. Transshipment-hub congestion is the under-covered disruption. Hormuz workarounds have jammed Singapore and Port Klang, rippling into Transpacific lanes with no direct Middle East exposure — a differentiated content angle.

Hormuz Is Reopening. Shipping's Old Playbook Isn't.

gCaptain · June 16, 2026 · Relevance 5/5
★★★★★

A deep analysis arguing the Cape of Good Hope stays the default container routing even with an interim deal, because Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM and Hapag-Lloyd won't resume normal Hormuz/Red Sea transit until safety is proven.
